TCP

This page provides information about the Transmission Control Protocol (TCP).

  • TCP: A protocol used for reliable data transmission over a network.
  • Reliable: Ensures that data is transmitted without errors and in the correct order.
  • Flow Control: Manages the rate of data transmission to prevent overwhelming the receiver.
